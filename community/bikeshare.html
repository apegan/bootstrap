Wellesly College Bike Share

During my time at Wellesley College, I oversaw the bike share system on campus. This included software and hardware, managing users, maintaining bikes, and providing bike related events and activities on campus. Broken and missing bikes led me to build accountability into our bike share system. I worked with the key management system and the college's IT Department to resolve server issues regarding automated email notifications, as well as tracked unusual user behaviors to improve the program. I developed a fine system and gathered feedback from users to structure an acceptable procedure. Some users had busy schedules, so returning a bike after 24 hours was inconvenient. I headed another program called the Long Term Bike Rental for a semester long rentals. Other initiatives included winter bike storage, bike maintenance workshops, and bike pedal festivals.
