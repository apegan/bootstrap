CultureHouse

Collaborated with Aaron Greiner to co-found CultureHouse, an "indoor public park" or "communal living room" that is free and open to all. Inspired to create a multi-functional public space to work, chill, and have fun with regular scheduled events. We applied and secured grant funding to test several pop-up outdoor spaces around Greater Boston area. Through various partnerships including BetterBlock and Olin College of Engineering, was able to successfully build and test the idea to grow the organization. With limited funds, we built outdoor poster signs, benches, tables, and activies to engage passerbys. In addition to collecting feedback from community members of the type of community space they wished for, we also mapped people's path. We found that with our pop-up, more people engaged with the space and stayed longer outdoors.
