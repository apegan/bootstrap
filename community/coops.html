Housing Cooperatives

Living and learning from others in a cooperative environment is incredibly filling for me. I grew up in a tight-knit Buddhist community, and immediately sought out a communal living situation in college. In 2015, I joined Wellesley's sustainanbility housing cooperative named SCoop with 13 other people. In addition to common coop norms, I contributed to the community by bringing diversity and inclusion consultants. Living with a diverse group of people posed challenges, and third-party facilitator were able to identify cultural norms we could not see. After Wellesley, I helped start two other housing cooperatives in Somerville and Boston. Both coops were intentional living situations with a mission to be a welcoming space by hosting events and celebrations.
