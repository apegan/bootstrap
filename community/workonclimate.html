Work on Climate - Climate Justice 101 Guide

I supported the inital creation of an online climate action community called Work on Climate. The slack group was started by Eugene Kirpichov and Cassandra Xia to encourage other professionals to enter or support the climate field. To help community engagement and connection, I set-up the beta version of a braintrust to help members find people with common interests. To strengthen core bonding, I also developed a communication and working style survey. The results were shared with the group along with a series of activity to improve team health. In November 2020, I joined the climate justice learning group to develop a guidebook on key topics for new learners. The first version of the guidebook will be released March 2021.
