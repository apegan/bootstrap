Rail Transport Analysis in MA

During a GIS class, I investigated where railroad owners should consider repairing railways to reinvest in local communities. By overlaying data layers for population decline from 1980-2019 with rail activity, I found that key communities were Medford, Weymouth, Brockton, New Bedford, and Chicopee. Data was provided by mass.gov.
