Wellesley Renewable Energy Feasibility Study

At the request of Wellesley Collegeâ€™s Office of Sustainability, the Environmental Studies capstone class researched and developed strategies for the future of energy on campus. My biggest contribution was designing the final report as well as calculating the cost per kilowatt-hour for every energy scenario, using the levelized cost of energy formula. LCOE facilitates cost comparisons across energy generation sources for the entire lifetime of each generator. It aggregates the capital cost, capacity factory, fixed costs of operations, variable costs of operations, heat rate, fuel cost, electricity price, and cost escalation rate. I also researched and scored geothermal and solar water heater scenarios, using SimaPro, a lifecycle analysis software, to determine the score of metrics like ecosystem distruption, global warming, carcinogens, non-carcinogens, respiratory, and ecotoxicity.
