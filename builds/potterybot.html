The Po(E)ttery Bot is a class project for Principles of Engineering (PoE). I was on a team of 4 others at Olin College of Engineering to design a robot that can shape a cup or bowl when given a centered piece of clay. The bot is a computer-controled router that travels across two axels - up and down, and left to right. The computer is programmed to several different modes. Each mode carved a differnt shape. I was in charge of CAD and budget for the project.
