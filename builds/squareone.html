Cottage Village

Cottage Village is a intentional tiny house village located in Cottage Grove, Oregon. I joined the construction team for 3 months in 2019 to build 4 homes. The village is created by a non-profit calculated SquareOne Villages that aims to bring affordable housing across Oregon. Cottage Village is on a 1 acre located in a residential neighborhood with a total of 12 homes and community building. I supported the construction starting from the mud-sill to insulation. I learned about subflooring, framing, installing rafters, sheathing, installing windows and doors, roofting, and insulating.
