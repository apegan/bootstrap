Homemade Bed Set

In 2018, I was determined to make my own bed frame for my tiny 7x10 sq ft room in Boston. The bed had in-set legs, slats, storage boxes on wheels, and a bench. The cuts were calculated so they could get cut at the hardware store (I didn't have power tools at the time). Joinery was simple using screws. Constructed using cedar and birch wood, and used mineral oil for finish. Took around 5 hours to design and plan, and a weekend to complete. Cost: $150.
